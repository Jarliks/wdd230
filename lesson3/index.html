Purpose

The goal of the Chamber of commerce site is to promote the business associates and a healthy business climate within the area. It will serve as a way to attract business and tourists to the area. Additionally, it will provide ways for people to sign up as members, and participate with the organization as members in a variety of ways.

Domain Name

The domain will be gilbertcity.com. It is direct, to the point, and ideal for searches.
